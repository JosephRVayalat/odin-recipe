Chicken Stew

Back to Home

Chicken stew: a warm, comforting dish featuring tender chicken pieces simmered with a medley of vegetables like carrots, potatoes, and onions, all bathed in a rich, savory broth. Seasoned with herbs and spices, it's a versatile, one-pot meal perfect for cozy evenings and family gatherings, offering a hearty and satisfying experience.

  • Chicken: (bone-in or boneless, thighs, drumsticks, or breasts)
  • Onions
  • carrots
  • potatoes
  • Celery
  • Chicken broth
  • Garlic
  • Olive oil or butter
  • Herbs
  • Salt and pepper
  1. Brown Chicken: Season chicken, then brown it in a pot with oil. Take it out.
  2. Cook Veggies: Cook chopped onions, carrots, and celery in the same pot.
  3. Add Broth & Chicken: Pour in chicken broth, add the chicken back in, and add potatoes.
  4. Simmer: Cook until chicken and veggies are tender.
  5. Thicken (Optional): Mix flour with water, stir into the stew to thicken.
  6. Chicken broth
  7. Add Peas (Optional): Add peas at the end
  8. Serve with bread and enjoy
